Meatwad Recipe

Meatwad is a very easy character to translate into real life. After all, he
is simply a ball of raw beef.

I do not suggest eating this dish, leave it for garnish and do not let it
touch the actual cooked food.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef

Instructions:

  1. Take your preferred amount of ground beef and roll it into a ball.
  2. Do not cook, do not eat, maybe serve. Enjoy!
